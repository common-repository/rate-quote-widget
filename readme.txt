The Rate Quote Widget improves your use of your time. It identifies leads that are ready, willing and qualified to be customers.

== Description ==

A famous MIT study proved what effective salespersons have always known -- when you contact a lead quickly, your odds of conversion increase. You've got 5 minutes to make that first contact in today's Internet Age. You can't meet demands like that. But we can.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1.  Download the plugin to your local hard drive
2.  Use the "Plugins > Upload" menu to install the plugin
3.  Configure the plugin from Dashboard > Rate Quote Widget
4.  Place the form anywhere on your blog using the Appearance > Widget menu

** What does the Rate Quote Widget cost? **

Rate Quote Widget is 100% free.

Different users get different results depending on website traffic and marketing. When your leads come in, though, you'll know which ones to work, and which ones to ignore.

** How long until the Rate Quote Widget is live on my site? ** 

Immediately, if you're comfortable with WordPress plugins.

** How do I get customer support? ** 

Rate Quote Widget is written, maintained and published by Dan Green, an active loan officer. The product is free, so support is thin. Yes. During your first week with the Rate Quote Widget, you will receive automated emails which show different ways to use Rate Quote Widget. These emails may help bridge some gaps for you. However, if you'd like to contract with someone for customized consulting work, please just ask.
